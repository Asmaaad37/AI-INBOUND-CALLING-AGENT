Inbound Calling Agent

An intelligent AI-powered system designed to handle inbound calls in Urdu and English, understand user intent and emotions, and provide real-time responses or escalate to human agents when required.

Core Features

Urdu Speech Recognition

Accurate ASR supporting Urdu and mixed Urdu-English conversations.

Intent & Emotion Detection

Advanced NLU models detect intent, urgency, and emotional tone.

Knowledge Graph Reasoning

GraphRAG-based reasoning ensures contextual and consistent answers.

Human Escalation

Seamless call transfer with full conversation context.
